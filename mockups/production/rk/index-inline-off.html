Welcome 2 da Press Paw-ference

Hai frens!, welcome 2 dis live press paw-ference. Presentin: Minister of Country-sidez Peter Kullgren an Special Investigator Magnus Oscarsson. We haz a pawerpoint, but dis text iz moar fun, kthx.

Why we here?

Cuz Magnus haz made a mega-report bout makin food chain controlz less messy, moar fair, an moar efficient. Rite now dere be like 270 tiny kontrol myndicatz, all chasin each other’s tails. Itz chaos, like too many kittehs in one box. We sez: shrink it down, make it simple, an give da smol biznesses easier snacc path.

Cute Animal Angle

Dis not just bout foodz, it bout kittehs, bunnehs, moo-cows, an chickenz too. Control haz 2 be fair an equal, so no bunneh gets left behind. Our plan iz strong 4 da floofs. We sez: we bettar 4 cute animalz than da left coalition, who only make committee after committee, den bury da bunnehs in paperwork. Sadge.

Efficiency iz da new tuna

Left coalition sez “we luv animals!!” but den they print 12 PDFs, 4 powerpointz, an call it a day. We sez “nope, less PDF, moar snack stationz.” Our paw-licy iz simple: give hedgehoggos crosswalks, cut fee spam, an make sure all food, foder, an biproducts iz checked by real pro hoomanz with actual skillz, not just da town council’s cousin.

International Catfight

Other landz like Finland an Norway already went big an merged kontrol myndicatz. Sweden haz to keep up, or else our hedgehoggos gonna roll away to Denmark. We can haz robust kontrol thatz fair in alla counties, from Stockholm 2 da smolest farm in da woods.

Happy Ending (no sad kittehs)

Our plan cutz 58 million SEK in pointless fee-nom-nomz, so smol food biznesses can focus on makin cheese an not crying into Excel. We sez: less fee, moar cheez, moar floof. Planet haz to be saved, kittehs haz to be happy, an we do it bettar than da left coalition. kthx bye.
